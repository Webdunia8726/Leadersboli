About The Post

Lucknow    Work from office.



LeadersBoli is looking for a talented Video Editor to join our creative team. This role is
crucial for bringing our inspiring stories to life through compelling video content. The
ideal candidate will have a keen eye for detail, a passion for storytelling and proficiency
in video editing software.

Key Responsibilities

  • Edit raw footage into polished, engaging videos that align with LeadersBoli’s mission and vision.
  • Collaborate with the content and production teams to develop video concepts and storyboards
  • Handle all aspects of post-production, including color correction, sound editing, and adding special effects
  • Ensure all videos meet high-quality standards and maintain consistency in style and branding.

    • Qualifications

  • Education - Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Communications, Digital Media or a related fields.
  • Experiance - Minimum of 2-3 years of experience in digital media, social media management or a related role.
  • Skills -
    • Proficiency in digital content creation tools (Adobe Creative Suite, Canva, etc.)
    • Strong understanding of SEO and web traffic metrics.
    • Excellent written and verbal communication skills.
    • Creativity and attention to detail.
    • Ability to multitask and work under tight deadlines
