Our Reports and Research are Designed to Offer in-depth Analysis and Insights on various aspects of Youth Leadership, Empowerment, and Development. These Resources aim to Inform, Educate, and Inspire the Next Generation of Leaders.


Our Reports


Browse through our collection of comprehensive reports that cover various topics related to youth leadership and development.

Start Up Fundings

A startup is a newly established company aiming to develop unique products or services, typically driven by innovation, rapid growth potential, and the pursuit of scalable business models.

Pradhan Mantri Vikas Kausal Yojna

Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana (PMKVY) is a skill development initiative by the Indian government aimed at empowering youth with industry-relevant skills to enhance employability and foster entrepreneurship.

NAPS-2

The NAPS Scheme-2, launched by the Indian government, aims to provide pension benefits to workers in the unorganized sector. It offers a fixed monthly pension, contributing to financial security post-retirement. This scheme supports informal sector workers, ensuring they have a reliable source of income in their later years.


Research Studies



Explore our detailed research studies that provide data-driven insights on youth empowerment and leadership.

Technology and Innovation

Technology and innovation shape the future by advancing fields like artificial intelligence and automation. Youth can prepare by learning and adapting to these changes for successful careers.

Mental Health and Well-being

Mental health and well-being involve understanding and managing emotions, stress, and mental disorders. It's crucial for youth to prioritize mental health, seek support, and practice self-care for a balanced life.

Digital Literacy and Online Safety

Digital literacy and online safety are crucial for today's youth. Understanding how to use technology responsibly and protecting personal information online ensures safe and informed internet use.


Case Studies



Learn from real-world examples of successful youth leadership initiatives and programs.

Educational Technology and Learning Outcomes

Educational technology, like apps and online tools, helps students learn better by making lessons more engaging, interactive and personalized, leading to improved academic performance and increased motivation.

Youth Employment and Career Development

Youth employment and career development studies show how career counseling and internships boost job readiness and long-term career success for young people transitioning from education to the workforce.

Youth Activism and Social Change

Youth activism drives social change by empowering young people to advocate for issues like climate action, equality and justice. Their efforts can influence policies and raise awareness globally.
